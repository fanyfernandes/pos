What's a CD-P?

The aespa CD-P is a limited edition of the group's first full-length album, Armageddon, released in 2024. This special version features a compact, retro-inspired CD player designed to align with aespa's multiverse concept within the SM Culture Universe (SMCU). The design draws inspiration from the "KWANGYA" universe, reflecting aespa's narrative that transcends the Real and Digital Worlds into the multiverse.â€‹

What's Inside the CD-P Version?

  • A functioning CD player with a unique design
  • A CD-R containing the album's tracks
  • A lyric sheet
  • Five photo cards
  • Ten stickers inspired by the album's concept

Why It's Special

This edition offers fans a tangible and interactive way to experience aespa's music, merging physical media with the group's immersive storytelling. The CD-P version was highly anticipated and sold out rapidly upon release, highlighting its appeal among fans and collectors.
